Welcome to Greencrate

Recipes and ingredients sent straight to your door.

All for only $5 per meal*

Register and Order

After registering, you can let us know which days you want a meal kit, and at what time (in between 4-7 pm). You can also let us know what cutlery and cooking materials you do and don't have, so we can tailor our meals to be accessible to you! If you are missing some main cooking materials, we can send you some for free with your first kit.

Assemble and Prepare

On our end, we bulk purchase healthy and affordable foods from farmers, lowering costs for both us and you! From our menu designs, we create food kits for each day, containing ingredients for our recipe of the day, and a recipe card with detailed instructions on how to create a beautiful, awesome meal!

Transport and Deliver

We conveniently deliver the meal kit to your house or place of residence at whatever time (between 4-7 pm) and day you choose. We eliminate the time needed to head to several grocery stores, as well as transporation costs. In addition, the kits are reusable, so once you're done we'll pick up your leftover boxes!

Cook and Enjoy

After receiving the kit, you can cook each recipe (with the included recipe cards) in less than 30 minutes and using only a few basic materials. We believe that you can learn some pretty rocking awesome cooking skills! Once you're done, you'll enjoy a healthy and delicious meal that you prepared yourself!

Why choose Greencrate?

Affordable

Greencrate offers you a different meal every day of the week, for only $5. By delivering the meal straight to where you live, we can help you save money on groceries and transportation.

Educational

Every meal kit that we offer contains the ingredients needed to make the meal and a recipe card. We believe that this is a great way to learn how to create healthy, nutritious, and easy meals.

Environmentally Friendly

The boxes that contain your meal kit are reusable instead of disposable, which helps reduce waste. By bulk buying excess produce from farmers, as well as giving you all the necessary ingredients so that you don't accidentally buy too much food, Greencrate helps reduce food waste.

Healthy

The food kits that we provide you with contain all the necessary ingredients to cook a healthy meal. Each one of these kits contains only fresh produce.

Quick

All the meals we send you take 30 minutes or less to prepare. Instead of picking up the meal, we save you time by delivering a meal kit straight to your house at any time between 4 to 7 pm.
